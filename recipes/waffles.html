Waffles

Hit the spot Waffles

You're sure to bring a crowd with these irresistible waffles!

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Whisk some stuff, (Look at me I am highighted!) add some other stuff.
  2. Pour the batter onto the waffle iron. Cook until golden brown.
