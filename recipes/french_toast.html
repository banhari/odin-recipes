Fluffy French Toast

This french toast is light and fluffy and oh-so satisfying!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 12 slices bread

Steps

  1. Mix all the things!
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in mixture.
  4. Cook bread until golden brown on each side.
